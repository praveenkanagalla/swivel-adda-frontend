About Swivel Adda

Welcome to Swivel Adda – where every cup sparks a conversation.

At Swivel Adda, we believe tea and coffee are more than just drinks—they're invitations to pause, connect, and savor the moment. Whether you're steeping a delicate oolong or brewing a bold espresso, every sip is a ritual. And every ritual deserves a space—an adda—to be enjoyed.

Our Story

Born from the desire to create a digital café experience, Swivel Adda is a space where flavors, stories, and cultures meet. The word adda—a gathering place for friends and ideas—inspires everything we do. Whether you're working from home, catching up with a friend, or enjoying a quiet morning, we’re here to make those moments richer.

What We Serve

  • Handpicked Tea - From serene green teas to bold masala chais, our blends are crafted for every mood.
  • Craft Coffees - Ethically sourced, expertly roasted, and freshly ground for that perfect brew.
  • Brew Gear & Accessories - Mugs, kettles, and everything you need to complete your corner café.
